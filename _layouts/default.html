about me

By day, I'm a Senior Developer with Readify in Sydney, Australia. I work with clients using technologies including ASP.NET, WPF, Silverlight, WP7 and now Windows 8 - and help development teams to make the most of these technologies.

In my spare time I also help co-ordinate the Code52 open source crew - an opportunity for people to learn and contribute to open source projects.

I was recently awarded an MVP award from the ASP.NET/IIS teams for work with the community.

When you can tear me away from a computer, you'll likely find me hanging out at community events - sometimes up the front presenting, sometimes up the back heckling, and sometimes holding down the bar at the after party.
